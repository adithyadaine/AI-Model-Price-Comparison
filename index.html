About LLM Ledger

Compare AI model pricing, performance, and capabilities in one place.

Transparent Pricing

View up-to-date input and output costs per 1 million tokens for all major LLMs. No hidden fees or complex calculations.

Context Windows

Compare context window sizes to find the right model for your document processing and long-context tasks.

Visual Analytics

Use interactive charts to visualize price trends and compare models side-by-side.

Data Source

Data is sourced from provider documentation, Artificial Analysis, and OpenRouter to ensure accuracy.

Designed for developers, researchers, and businesses to make informed decisions about AI infrastructure.

Privacy & Data

Your privacy is paramount. LLM Ledger is designed to be a secure, client-side tool.

No Data Collection

Since no login is required, data collection is out of the question. We do not track search queries or usage metrics.

Local Processing

All data filtering, sorting, and comparisons are performed entirely within your browser. No data is sent to external servers.

External Links

Links to model providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) take you to third-party sites. Please review their respective privacy policies.

Transparency

This application is built with transparency in mind, ensuring you can verify how your data is handled.

Trust and security are core to the LLM Ledger experience.
